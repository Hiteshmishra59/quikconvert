Age Calculator

Find your exact age in years, months, and days

About Age Calculator

Our age calculator gives you your exact age in years, months, and days based on your date of birth. It also shows you bonus stats like total days lived, total weeks, and how many days until your next birthday — all in seconds.

How to Use

Simply select your date of birth using the date picker and click Calculate Age. The tool will instantly display your precise age broken down into years, months, and days, along with extra stats.

Why Use This Age Calculator

Unlike a simple subtraction, this calculator accounts for leap years and varying month lengths to give you a truly precise result. It's perfect for filling out forms, verifying eligibility, celebrating milestones, or just satisfying your curiosity.
