Privacy Policy

We built QuikConvert to be simple and trustworthy — no accounts, no tracking of personal data.

🔒 No Login Required

QuikConvert does not require you to create an account or log in to use any of its tools. All tools — including the calculator, converter, currency, QR generator, SIP, EMI, image resizer, and document converter — are fully accessible without registration.

📋 No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII). Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any contact details.
  • We do not store the values you enter into any calculator or converter.
  • Files you upload for image resizing or document conversion are processed locally in your browser (browser tools) or temporarily on the server for Office/PDF conversions — they are never stored or logged.
  • We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

📊 Google Analytics

QuikConvert uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the website. This service collects anonymised data such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General geographic region (country/city level)
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Referral source (how you arrived at the site)

This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally. Google Analytics is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

🔗 Third-Party Links

QuikConvert may contain links to third-party websites (for example, exchange rate APIs, font services, or other resources). These external sites have their own privacy policies, and we have no control over their content or practices.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of any third-party websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any external site you visit.

🌐 External APIs

Some tools use external APIs to function:

  • Currency Converter — fetches live exchange rates from ExchangeRate-API. Your IP address may be visible to their servers as part of the HTTP request, subject to their own privacy policy.
  • QR Generator — uses QR Server API to render QR codes. The text you enter for QR generation is sent to their servers to generate the image.

We recommend reviewing the respective privacy policies of these services if you have concerns.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of QuikConvert after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
