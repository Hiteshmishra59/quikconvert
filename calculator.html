About This Calculator

Our free online calculator helps you perform fast and accurate calculations. It supports basic arithmetic operations — addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division — as well as scientific functions like square (x²), square root (√), and percentage. Designed for quick results and ease of use, it works on all devices with no installation needed.

How to Use

Click the buttons or use your keyboard to enter numbers and operators. Press = to see the result instantly. Use ⌫ to delete the last digit, C to clear everything, x² to square the current value, and √ to find the square root. For longer expressions, use the Basic Calculator panel on the right.
