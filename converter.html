About Unit Converter

Our unit converter supports over 20 categories — including length, weight, temperature, time, area, volume, speed, energy, data storage, and more. Whether you need to convert kilometers to miles, Celsius to Fahrenheit, or megabytes to gigabytes, this tool handles it all instantly and accurately.

How to Use

Select a category from the chips above, then choose your "From" and "To" units from the dropdowns. Enter the value you want to convert and click Convert. Use the ⇄ swap button to quickly reverse the conversion direction.

Why Use This Converter

With 20+ unit categories and hundreds of conversion pairs, this is the only tool you'll ever need for unit conversions. It works offline, requires no signup, and gives accurate results for scientific, academic, and everyday conversions alike.
