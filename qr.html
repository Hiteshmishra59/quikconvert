About QR Code Generator

Our free QR code generator creates scannable QR codes from any text, URL, phone number, or message in seconds. Choose from four size options (128 px to 512 px) and download the QR code as a PNG image to use anywhere. Use batch mode to generate up to 20 QR codes at once.

How to Use

Type or paste your text or URL in the input field, select your preferred QR code size, and click Generate QR Code. Once generated, you can download the PNG file or copy the direct image URL. Switch to Batch Mode to generate multiple QR codes at once from a list of URLs or texts.

Why Use This QR Generator

No signup, no watermark, and completely free. This tool is perfect for creating QR codes for business cards, product packaging, social media links, event check-ins, and more. The batch mode makes it ideal for marketing campaigns and bulk QR generation.
