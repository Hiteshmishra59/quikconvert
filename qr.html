About QR Code Generator

Our free QR code generator creates scannable QR codes from any text, URL, phone number, or message in seconds. Choose from four size options (128px to 512px) and download the QR code as a PNG image to use anywhere.

How to Use

Type or paste your text or URL in the input field, select your preferred QR code size, and click Generate QR Code. Once generated, you can download the PNG file or copy the direct image URL to use in your projects.

Why Use This QR Generator

No signup, no watermark, and completely free. This tool is perfect for creating QR codes for business cards, product packaging, social media links, event check-ins, and more. The generated QR codes are high-quality and readable by all major QR scanner apps.
